About Us

At Koil Creations, we believe fashion is more than clothing

Koil Creations was born in 2010, founded by a visionary young fashionpreneur, Oluwaseun Kuforiji. The name “KOIL” reflects the personal connection and legacy of its founder, as it is derived from the first letters of his full name: Kuforiji Oluwaseun Imoleayo Lawrence.

Since its inception, Koil Creations has grown into a premium fashion brand with a mission to blend cultural heritage with contemporary design. With offices in Nigeria and the UK, we create timeless pieces that celebrate individuality and tell stories of creativity, innovation, and excellence.

Our founder, Oluwaseun Kuforiji, brings over a decade plus of experience in fashion design and garment making. His dedication to craftsmanship has established Koil Creations as a brand known for its attention to detail, use of luxurious fabrics, and ability to craft garments that empower individuals to express their unique identity.

Our Mission & Goal

At Koil Creations, we believe fashion is more than clothing—it’s a way to preserve heritage, reclaim narratives, and inspire confidence. Join us on this journey as we redefine the future of fashion, one stitch at a time.

  • Custom-tailored clothing for men, women, and children
  • Ready-to-wear collections for every occasion
  • Fashion training programs to inspire and nurture the next generation of designers
